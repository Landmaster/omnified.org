THECC

Helping you get where you want to be

The College Communiqué is an online newsletter dedicated to providing SAT/ACT studying techniques and test taking tips by high scorers. Within this newsletter, there are all commentary columns and fun articles written by previous test takers. All editors and writers have taken the SAT/ACT before and have scored above the 96 percentiles. The College Communiqué aims to help bright and motivated students who want to achieve high scores in order to increase the likely hood of them matriculating into a college of their choice. The college Communiqué also provides advice on how to apply to colleges, how to present yourself at interviews and how to craft a great application.
