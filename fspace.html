More than a Makerspace

F[Space] Is a Makerspace/Hackerspace based in downtown Frederick, MD. On top of providing various fabrication/prototyping resources, F[Space] seeks to be a collaboration space. One where STEM communities throughout Maryland can share projects and help educate others of their endeavors. F[Space] also hosts monthly talks at Hood College about various topics, from robotics to lockpicking.
